About Project Happier

Smile! Smile! Smile! The Project Happier is to make you smile for the day. Click on the item you love- that makes you smile, brings joy and happiness when you see it and motivates you for the day. Choose dog if you are a dog person, choose cat if you are a cat person, or choose quote to get some motivational quote for the day to get going through your rough day.

The project is developed by Sujan Dhakal.
